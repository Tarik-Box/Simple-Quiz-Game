    About Quiz Game

    The Quiz Game offers a fun and dynamic way to test your knowledge across different categories and levels. Each level delivers a balanced mix of challenge and entertainment — from quick general questions to specific topics that make you think twice before choosing your answer. It’s not just about being right; it’s about learning something new every time you play. Choose your level, trust your instincts, and see how far your knowledge can take you!
